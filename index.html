About

RiskCube — Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) exists to advance how organisations understand, measure, and manage digital risk.

We serve risk and compliance leaders, boards, and practitioners who need clarity and practical frameworks in a fast-changing digital landscape.

  • Evidence-based research and thought leadership
  • Training and certification programmes
  • Advisory and capability building

Case Study

How we helped a financial services firm align digital risk with board reporting.

Problem

The board and executive team lacked a shared view of digital risk. Reporting was siloed and inconsistent, and the link between digital initiatives and risk appetite was unclear.

Approach

We designed a lightweight digital risk taxonomy and reporting framework, ran workshops with risk, technology, and business leads, and piloted a single board-ready dashboard.

Outcome

Within six months, the firm had a consistent narrative on digital risk at board level, clearer ownership, and a roadmap to integrate the framework into existing risk and control processes.
